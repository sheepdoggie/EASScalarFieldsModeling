# v0.1.43 Whole-Field Conjugate-Vacuum Derived-Longitudinal Packet

Status: EXPLORATORY MECHANISM DISCOVERY ONLY.

v0.1.43 adds a whole-field runner for the question whether vacuum first-association splitting into generated conjugate branches, with one association of each branch to its conjugate under selected variants, can yield a zero longitudinal condition as a derived residual rather than as a stored path-facing scalar value.

The runner starts from undefined whole-field vacuum sites, performs first-association conjugate splitting, applies whole-field SOO, and reads post-run records using lambda(P)=Phi(P)-Phi(a_path(P)), tau+(P)=Phi(P), and tau-(P)=Phi(a_conjugate(P)).

Forbidden generator inputs include stored path-facing zero scalar values, component-zero sealing, constructed photon endpoints, q/-q initializers, photon labels, Standard Model role labels, target Delta L, and endpoint-class comparison sets.

Approval authorizes exploratory use only. It does not certify photons, charge, bounded supports, Standard Model particles, or a path-accommodation theorem.
